Projects

Our home projects are concentrated around IoT sensing systems. We have designed, constructed and programmed several different weather stations. One of them is on the roof of our house and we call it 'Swallow', the second is hanging outside the window and we call it 'Bee'.

So, why? Why do we do this?

Firstry, just because it is incredibly fun=)

Secondly, because you can try a lot of interesting sensors and modules and design PCB's in a way you want without bothering too much about cost (thanks to support of our company Third Pin LLC , where we used to work for about 5 years).

And last but not the least, because you can collect a lot of data from these devices in order to use different modern platforms, such as Graphana, to visualize it, what is far to fascinatingly and, actually, usefull.

About

Story

Hi! We are Dima and Anastasiia - the family which is highly interested in everything related to IT. We complement each other perfectly: one of us is a hardware engineer, the other is a software specialist. Guess who is who=)

Our background is about 5 years in Electronics Department at Third Pin LLC . In those years we have got a lot of experience, complete many different projects, from funny LED blinkers and homemade "just for fun" devices to huge commercial projects such as navy control panels and telemetry systems. Our work for us, isn't only work - it is also a hobby.
